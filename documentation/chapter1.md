# Chapter One - Introduction


## 1.1 Background of Study

The advancement of Artificial Intelligence (AI) has significantly influenced various fields of engineering, including structural engineering. Traditional structural design methods often involve manual calculations and the use of specialized software tools, which can be time-consuming and require a high level of expertise.

This project explores the application of AI in structural engineering by developing a system capable of assisting in beam design calculations and generating structural outputs.

The project aims to leverage AI techniques to automate structural design processes, enhance accuracy, and provide intelligent recommendations for engineers and students in the field of structural engineering.

The system will be designed to accept user inputs, process structural parameters, and generate both numerical and graphical outputs, including Shear Force and Bending Moment diagrams.

## 1.2 Problem Statement

Manual structural design processes can be repetitive, time-consuming, and prone to human error. There is a need for an intelligent system that can assist in automating structural design calculations while maintaining accuracy.

There is therefore a need for a system that can assist engineers and students by automating structural calculations and providing intelligent design recommendations.

The project will focus on the design of reinforced concrete beams under simple loading conditions, with the aim of improving efficiency and accuracy in structural design processes.

The system will be developed to accept user inputs, process structural parameters, and generate both numerical and graphical outputs, including Shear Force and Bending Moment diagrams, to aid in the design of reinforced concrete beams.


## 1.3 Aim and Objectives

### Aim:
To develop an AI-assisted system for beam design that can automate structural calculations, provide intelligent design recommendations, and generate graphical outputs such as Shear Force and Bending Moment diagrams.

### Objectives:
- To compute structural design parameters
- To develop a dataset for training an AI model
- To train an AI model for predicting design outputs
- To implement prompt-based input for parameter extraction
- To generate structural design recommendations
- To visualize structural diagrams such as Shear Force and Bending Moment


## 1.4 Scope of Study

This project focuses on the design of reinforced concrete beams under simple loading conditions. The system will accept user inputs, process structural parameters, and generate both numerical and graphical outputs.

The project will involve the development of an AI model trained on a dataset generated from beam design calculations. The model will be used to predict design outputs based on user inputs. The system will also include a prompt-based input mechanism for parameter extraction and will generate structural design recommendations based on the calculated parameters. The project will also include the visualization of structural diagrams such as Shear Force and Bending Moment diagrams to aid in the design process.
